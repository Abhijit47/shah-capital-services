At Shah Capital Services, we are committed to maintaining the highest standards of integrity, transparency, professionalism, and ethical conduct in all client interactions and business activities.

Our principles include:

- Acting in the best interest of clients
- Maintaining honesty and transparency in all communications
- Providing suitable and need-based financial solutions
- Ensuring confidentiality of client information
- Complying with applicable regulatory guidelines and industry standards
- Avoiding misleading statements, false promises, or misrepresentation
- Maintaining professionalism, fairness, and accountability in all dealings

We believe in building long-term client relationships through trust, disciplined financial
guidance, and responsible service practices.

All employees, associates, and representatives of Shah Capital Services are expected to
adhere to these principles while carrying out their professional responsibilities.
