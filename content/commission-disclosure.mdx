**_Shah Capital Services_** acts as a distributor for various financial and insurance products and may receive commissions, brokerage, incentives, trail commissions, or other forms of remuneration from product manufacturers, asset management companies, insurance providers, or financial institutions for the distribution and servicing of such products.

---

These commissions are paid directly by the respective product providers and do not generally result in additional direct charges to clients unless specifically disclosed otherwise.

---

The compensation received may vary depending on the product category, investment amount, tenure, and product structure. Such compensation may create potential conflicts of interest; however, Shah Capital Services aims to maintain a client-centric approach focused on suitability, transparency, and long-term relationship management.

---

Clients are encouraged to review all product documents, scheme information documents, policy wordings, and disclosure materials carefully before investing.
